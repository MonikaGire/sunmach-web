Sunmach is an ISO 9001 : 2015 certified Manufacturer, Exporter & Supplier of SS 304H Special Machined Components over a decade.

At Sunmach, we are proud manufacturers of Hot Forged SS 304H Special Machined Components in Standard Sizes & Special Custom Made Sizes and Shapes. We are one of the most respected hot forgers and producers of high-integrity Special Machined Components in the Asia. The SS 304H Special Machined Components we provide are durable and high quality. Making the use of all the advanced technologies for Exotic Metal Special Machined Components, our highly experienced skilled team of professional combine hot induction forging machinery with CNC precision, automatic and manual machines and deploy the best precisely matching the client’s expectations.

As SS 304H has the best thermal, electrical, magnetostrictive and mechanical properties which make it resistant to a wide range of corrosive media and Sunmach frequently use it with alkalis.

SS 304H Special Machined Components Applications - SS 304H is utilized in food processing equipment where high product purity is required.
